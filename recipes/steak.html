Pan Cooked Steak

Description

This is a recipe for a very simple, easy, and quick pan cooked steak recipe. It uses simple flavors and a few ingredients to make a fantastic protien fast

This steak goes great with a potato or roasted vegetable side, My personal recommendations being oven roasted broccoli or crispy roasted potatoes.

  • 1-2 inch thick steak, good candidates are: NY strip, rib eye, filet mignon
  • Canola Oil (Or another high smoke point oil)
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat dry the steak with a paper towel
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper, The seasoning will help to form a tasty crust
  3. Turn on stovetop to a medium-high to high heat and let it sit until the pan is very hot
  4. While the stove heats up, peel the garlic and crush it under the flat end of a knife. no need to mince finely.
  5. Add oil to the pan, once the oil is shimmering and moving with a water consistency you know the pan is hot enough
  6. place the steaks into the pan, release away from you so that oil doesnt splatter you. You should hear the sizzling of the steaks
  7. tilt the pan so that the oil pools on one side, and then cook the edges of the steak for about 1 minute each side
  8. Lay the steaks flat and leave them alone! let them sit undisturbed for the crust to form. once it has (about 3 minutes) flip the steaks.
  9. Cook on the other side for about 3 minutes
  10. In the final minute of cooking, add the butter, whole Rosemary and Thyme, and the garlic cloves to the pan
  11. While its finishing, take a spoon and baste the steak in the butter/oil
  12. Remove the steaks and let them rest 5-10 minutes, cut against the grain or serve whole
