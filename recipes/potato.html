Crispy Roasted Potatoes

Description

This is a delicious side dish that pairs great with a pan cooked steak.

Similarly to the steak, the amount of ingredients for these potatoes is few. This recipe is a bit more complex, and needs more time to prepare. It's worth the extra effort though.

Ingredients

  • Russet or Yukon potatoes, cut into 1 inch chunks
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. Bring a pot of water up to a boil, add salt to the water.
  3. While the water is heating, take the garlic, rosemary, and olive oil, and cook it in a pan until the garlic starts to turn golden.
  4. Strain the oil mixture into a bowl
  5. once the water is boiling, add the potatoes. once the pot is back to a boil, reduce to a simmer and stir.
  6. Cook the potatoes until a knife can poke into the chunk easily, about 10 minutes of simmering
  7. Drain the potatoes, and then combine the oil, potatoes, and salt/pepper to taste in a mixing bowl. Shake the bowl roughly until the outside of the chunks has developed a layer of paste on the outside
  8. Add the potatoes to the oven for 20 minutes, then take a spatula to shake and turn the potatoes.
  9. Continue roasting the potatoes for 30-40 minutes longer, shaking occasionally.
  10. Take the finished potatoes and toss them in the cooked garlic/aromatics. add salt and pepper to taste. Serve Fresh.
